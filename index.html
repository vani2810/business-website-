About Ekoching

Shaping Future Leaders Since Day One

Ekoching is Ahmedabad's most trusted EdTech coaching institute, specialising in IPMAT, CAT, CLAT, and CUET preparation. Founded on the belief that every student deserves world-class guidance, we combine rigorous academics with personalised mentoring to unlock each learner's full potential.

Our faculty comprises IIM alumni, legal experts, and national-rank holders who don't just teach—they inspire. With small batch sizes and data-driven progress tracking, we make sure no student is left behind.

Our Mission

Make quality education accessible and empower students to secure seats in India's top institutions.

Our Vision

To be the most student-centric coaching platform that drives holistic academic and personal growth.
